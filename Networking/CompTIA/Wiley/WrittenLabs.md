## Chapter 1<br>Introduction to Networks
1. A ***LAN*** is a network typically found in your home or workplace.
2. A ***WAN*** connection connects your home or owrkplace to the Internet.
3. A ***start*** topology is also known as a hub-and-spoke toplogy.
4. A term ***demarc (demarcation point)*** is used to describe the service-related entry point for provider.
5. The ***Collapsed core*** model is used to condense the core and distribution layer into one layer.
6. A ***vNIC*** is a virtual piece of hardware that connects the virtual machine operating system to network.
7. Fibre Channel is typically found inside a ***SAN***.
8. The term used to reference the samllest peice of network is ***network segment***.
9. The ***MPLS*** technology uses lables to switch traffic.
10. The **spine-leaf** architecture is found in data centers to provide low-latency connectivity to servers.
